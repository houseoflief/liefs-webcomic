Acolyte, alternatively titled **I am not the god who brought you out of Egypt** is an illustrated version of a poem I first wrote in 2022 as part of a series of sonnets for a poetry writing course, and illustrated during a Comics Poetry workshop hosted by Jaromir Stoll and Zine Club Columbus.
